IN THE MAKING

At Craftika, we have a team of skillful craftsmen in their respective field. In order to
complete a beautiful metal statue, it takes combined effort of multiple individuals.
We follow lost wax method for making the statues which is one of the oldest and
finest methods in Nepal. Starting from making a wax statue to giving it a final touch
with face painting, it requires skills of various artisans. There is a process which needs,
to be followed in a systematic order like (in brief); casting, molding, hand carving, gold
plating and face painting. Each process is fulfilled by a specialized artist in that
making part. It takes about 3 to 4 weeks to complete an authentic metal statue.
